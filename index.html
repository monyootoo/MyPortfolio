Fueled by the vibrant color palettes of Valorant character design and Yatutatsu’s artwork, I crafted a visually striking website that reflects my personal brand. I juxtaposed a more retro pixel display font against a clean sans-serif to create a sense of personality and professionalism.

To ensure seamless user experience, I wireframed key components like my resume and skills section, prioritizing clarity and accessibility. The culmination of this process is a website that not only showcases my skills but also embodies my creative spirit.
